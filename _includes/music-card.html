I do not consider myself a creator in a specific musical genre, because I love music as such. As a producer, I have a part in several music projects, unfortunately I can't boast about them. For years I have been trying to explore and disassemble various musical genres, adding a piece of myself to them. I experiment by creating in a specific genre with non-specific tools. I approach each piece individually. Each time I make changes in the production process, trying to achieve "freshness" in stagnant sounds. I know that in this way I will not reach the ear of the mass audience, as this is accustomed to the "heard" sound imposed by commercial musical standards. I have hundreds of musical productions to my credit, a portion of which I present here.

Listen samples from my soundcloud TomekT.

Everything I've posted on soundcloud is experimentation from different musical genres. There is no style there, and the equipment I created on left a lot to be desired, making some bands (especially those between 400 Hz and 1.2 kHz) potentially over-represented. Publication dates do not coincide with production dates. This was mainly because health problems prevented me from making music for many years, which greatly affected my entire life. Productions that are reasonably fresh will be found directly on this page. I hope they will not harm you at all. :)
